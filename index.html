Introduction

This project was developed as part of the Knowledge Engineering for the Humanities course. It explores how SPARQL queries and Large Language Models can be used to enrich the Wikidata representation of the Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi.

Our goal is to identify missing semantic information and propose new RDF triples that represent the religious, cultural, and pilgrimage-related significance of the mausoleum.
